What Is It

Gemini Motion Lab

🎥

Record

Visitor records a short dance or movement clip on the kiosk tablet.

🤖

AI Transforms It

Gemini analyzes the movement and generates a personalized AI avatar video.

📱

Take It Home

Visitor scans a QR code to download their video and save to Apple / Google Wallet.

Why This Demo

Showcasing Gemini Multimodal

👁️

Video Understanding

Gemini 2.5 Pro watches the video and understands body movement, tempo, and style — not just static images.

🎨

Image Generation

Gemini 2.5 Flash generates a styled avatar portrait from a text description of the person.

🎬

Video Generation

Veo 3 brings the avatar to life — replicating the exact motion captured in the original clip.

Troubleshooting

Quick Runbook

SymptomLikely CauseFix
Stuck on "Processing" Veo job queued or slow Wait up to 90s. If >3 min, refresh kiosk.
"Queue Full" screen 3 jobs already running Normal — ask visitor to wait ~1 min.
QR doesn't load Share page still generating Wait 10s and scan again.
Blank / white screen App crash or network drop Hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R) or reload tab.
Kiosk stuck idle No activity for 30 min Auto-resets. Tap screen to restart manually.
Wallet button missing Avatar not ready yet Reload share page. Wallet shows when avatar done.
